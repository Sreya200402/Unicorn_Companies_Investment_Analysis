# 🦄 Unicorn Companies Investment Analysis

## 📌 Problem Statement

Analyze unicorn companies to identify high-potential investment opportunities based on valuation, funding, industry trends, and investor behavior.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To build an interactive Power BI dashboard that helps investors make data-driven decisions by evaluating:

* Valuation growth
* Funding efficiency
* Industry performance

---

## 📊 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel
* DAX

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Unicorn Companies Dataset
* Funding Dataset
* Investors Dataset

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* Investment opportunities are strongest in high-growth industries such as **FinTech** and **Internet Software**, particularly in **North America**.

* Companies with **high valuation and relatively lower funding** indicate efficient capital utilization and better investment potential.

* Investors should focus on industries with **consistent valuation growth** and strong investor backing.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Focus on high-growth industries such as FinTech, AI, and Internet Software
* Prioritize regions with strong startup ecosystems like North America
* Invest in companies with efficient funding-to-valuation ratios
